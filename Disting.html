Distinguishable Probability

1. If a family has two children, and one is a boy, what is the probability that both children are boys?



2. In a certain city, it is known that 20% of the people have blue eyes. If a person is selected at random from the city, what is the probability that the person has blue eyes, given that they are male?



3. A box contains 5 red balls and 3 green balls. If 2 balls are drawn from the box at random, what is the probability that both balls are green, given that the first ball drawn was red?



4. In a factory, 10% of the products are defective. If a batch of 5 products is selected at random, what is the probability that at most 2 of them are defective, given that the first product is defective?



5. A bag contains 10 marbles (4 red, 3 blue, and 3 green). A marble is randomly selected and not replaced. A second is randomly selected and not replaced. Then, a third is randomly selected. What is the probability of having no red?
