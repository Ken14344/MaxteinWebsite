Conditional Probability

1. A bag contains 10 red marbles and 8 blue marbles. If two marbles are drawn at random without replacement, what is the probability that both marbles are red given that the first marble drawn was red?



2. A multiple-choice test has 20 questions, each with four choices. If a student guesses on each question, what is the probability that the student answers at least 15 questions correctly given that the student answered the first 10 questions correctly?



3. A basketball player makes 80% of their free throws. If the player is given 10 free throws, what is the probability that they make exactly 7 of them given that they made the first 4 free throws?



4. A company produces two types of products, A and B. Product A has a 30% defective rate, while product B has a 20% defective rate. If a product is chosen at random and is found to be defective, what is the probability that it is product A?



5. A bag contains 4 black socks and 6 white socks. If two socks are drawn without replacement, what is the probability that the second sock is white, given that the first sock was black?
